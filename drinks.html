Sophia's Grotto

22 Birch Street . Roslindale, MA


617-323-4595

DRINKS

An array of red, white, rose and sparkling European wines
are offered by the glass, half carafe, full carafe or bottle.

The four beer options on draft are more regional, with Gritty's
Blackfly Stout and Victory Pilsner topping the list ($4.50/glass).

The bar offers its own specialty cocktails and martinis, including
the Pomegranate Royal, made of prosecco and PAMA,
a sweet pomegranate liqueur ($7.95).

Soft drinks and espresso are also available.
